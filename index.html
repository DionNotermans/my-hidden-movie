My favorite movie

In this magazine we are going to talk extensively about my favorite film, namely Cars the movie.
Various topics will be discussed, but more about that later.

Why the movie Cars?

I chose the movie Cars, because I watched it a lot as a child and I was reminded of this recently when my brother was watching it again.
That's what makes it such a good movie. It doesn't matter how old you are, it's still a fun movie for the whole family.

Several parts of the film have come out over the years.
Part 1 started with a racing car that ends up in a quiet city and through the different parts you see this racing car grow into a true champion.
